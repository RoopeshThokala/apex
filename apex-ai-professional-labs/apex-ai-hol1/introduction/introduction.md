# Introduction

## **What is APEX?**

Oracle APEX is an Enterprise AI and low-code application development platform for Oracle Database. APEX Application Development, Autonomous AI Data Warehouse (ADW), Autonomous AI Transaction Processing (ATP), and Autonomous AI JSON Database are fully managed services, pre-integrated and pre-configured with APEX, for rapidly building and deploying modern data-driven applications in Oracle Cloud. Business users, citizen, and application developers can create or generate intelligent enterprise apps they can trust with Oracle APEX

In this workshop, you learn to obtain an APEX workspace. Once you have an APEX workspace provisioned, you explore How to configure Generative AI Service in Oracle APEX.

Estimated Time: 10 minutes

### Objectives

In this workshop, you will:

- Obtain a free APEX development environment.

- Configure Generative AI Service in APEX.
